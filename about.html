Hey there, I'm Alex! I'm a product manager based in New York. I grew up in Markham, Ontario 🍁, came down for school, and never quite left the city.

My day job is building products. I like the messy middle between what people need, what looks right, and what's actually possible to ship. The rest of my time goes to a camera: photos on the street and on the road, and short films I cut at the kitchen table.

When I'm not doing either, I'm probably playing pickup: frisbee, badminton, softball, whatever's on, watching the Blue Jays, arguing that Citi Bike beats a cab, or in line for a Heytea grapefruit mango boom.

This site is a running archive of all of it. No themes, no templates, just the work and the wandering.

Got a shoot in mind, or a product problem worth talking through? I'd love to hear about it.
